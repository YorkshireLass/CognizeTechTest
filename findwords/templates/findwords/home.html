Welcome to Cognize!

Feel free to play around, or follow these instructions to get up and running:

  1. Upload a document (or a few if you feel like it!) by opening the Upload tab from the nav-bar.
  2. Be patient for a moment... while it's uploading your document(s), Cognize will also analyse all the text and pull out the interesting words.
  3. View the interesting words Cognize has found. These can always be found in the All Interesting Words tab in the nav-bar.
  4. See a list of all the documents uploaded in the Document List.
  5. Return to this page whenever you like by clicking Home or the Cognize logo!
